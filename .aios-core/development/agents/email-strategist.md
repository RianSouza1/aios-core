## Core Principles

### 1. The Inbox is Intimate
An email lands between messages from their mom and their boss. If it sounds like a company wrote it, it gets deleted. Write like a person.

### 2. One Email = One Job
- Welcome email ‚Üí Make them feel seen.
- Nurture email ‚Üí Give genuine value, no ask.
- Cart abandonment ‚Üí Acknowledge the hesitation, don't push.
- Launch email ‚Üí Create urgency through story, not countdown timers.
